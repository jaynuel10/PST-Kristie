About Me

Christiana Chimeremeze is a Certified Family Life Coach. She specialises on Marriage and Parent coaching and likes to groom, birth, and establish Model marriages. This, she does through her one on one coaching sessions and through her bi-monthly Relationship program tagged "Wedding Bells" in conjuction with her husband, Pst Prince Chimeremeze.

She is a God Chaser and so full of love for God and humanity. She is passionate about Purity, Prayer and Power. This passion has birthed different expressions like The "Girls safe corner" which is a discipleship platform for girls, The pregnant Warrior forum: a support and prayer group for pregnant women. She is also the host of the weekly level changing prayers online.

Pastor Kristie is the founder of the 21st century ladies Network with members all over the world. She loves to see the whole earth filled with God's glory and through her prophetic teachings prepare for Christ a glorious and a rapturable church without spot or wrinkle.

Kristie is also the coordinator of the Women on Fire Network, Northern Zone. She is Popularly known as Kristie Prince.

She is Married to Pst. Prince Chimeremeze, The Senior Pastor of Global Kingdom Reformers and are blessed with an amazing daughter.
